# Yasumaro - AI Browsing Logger

Yasumaro automatically builds your personal browsing database with AI summaries.

**What it does:**
- Saves pages you actually read (based on time spent and scroll depth)
- Generates AI summaries using your own API key (Gemini, OpenAI, Groq, local LLMs)
- Stores everything locally in SQLite — no external server
- Optionally syncs to Obsidian daily notes

**Privacy first:**
All your browsing history stays on your device. Your data is yours.

**Perfect for:**
- Researchers collecting articles
- Developers learning new technologies
- Business professionals tracking industry news
- Students building knowledge bases
